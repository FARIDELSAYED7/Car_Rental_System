3. RELATIONSHIPS SUMMARY
=========================================================
- Admin IS A User (Inheritance)
- Customer IS A User (Inheritance)
- Rental HAS A Customer (Association / Aggregation)
- Rental HAS A Car (Association / Aggregation)
- Invoice HAS A Rental (Association / Composition)
- Invoice HAS A Payment (Association / Composition)
- DataStore USES DatabaseHelper (Dependency)
- All GUI Classes USE models and DataStore (Dependency)
